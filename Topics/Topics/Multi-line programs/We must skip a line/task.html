By now, you're probably wondering, "How can we print a blank line in Python?" It's super easy! Just use the print() function without anything inside the parentheses.

Check this out:

print("How")
print()
print("are")
print("you?")

This will give you:

How

are
you?

Notice how we successfully left the second line blank!
